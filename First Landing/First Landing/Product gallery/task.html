Description

In this stage, you will show your products to the world with a gallery of images!

Start by creating a new div with an id of product after the home div . This div will contain two other divs, you can think of the container div as a row, and the two divs as columns. So, give them an id of col-1 and col-2.

The column divs will contain a div and an image for the content. The div has a second-level title and a paragraph tag. After this div, the image is a product image of your liking.

Here is an example of the structure:

<!-- ROW -->
<div id="product">
  <!-- COLUMN -->
  <div id="col-1">
    <div> <!-- content --> </div>
    <!-- image -->
  </div>
  <!-- COLUMN -->
  <div id="col-2">
    <div> <!-- content --> </div>
    <!-- image -->
  </div>
</div>

You can increase the number of div rows, but the tests will only check for the first one.

For the style of the product div, the display should be flex . So, set the width at 100% . For the style of the first and column divs, the texts should be centred, any overflows should be hidden. For the paragraph tags inside the content divs, the font size is 1.25rem; the font weight is 300.

The images should have a width of 100%. The border radius is 21px on the top and right, 0px on the bottom and left. In addition, they should have the src, title and alt attributes with the values of your liking.

The example has extra paddings and margins, you can choose to add them.

Objectives

In this stage, your website should have:

  • A new div with the id of product in the main;
  • Two div containers inside the home div with IDs of col1 and col2;
  • A div container inside the div column with h1 and p tags;
  • An img tag inside the div column;
  • All tags should have the style described above.
